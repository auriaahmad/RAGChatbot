Mitsubishi – GX Developer Training
It is a comprehensive course for learning the basic level programming of Mitsubishi PLC in GX Developer software. You will learn the logic designing of GX Developer from scratch in a short period. No hardware equipment is required to follow the course. Install GX Developer software on your PC and you are good to go. The fundamentals of GX Developer programming will be covered also. Including video lectures and solved examples.
Buy Course
Course Introduction
This course is for Electrical, Electronics, Mechanical, or Mechatronics Engineers and Diploma holders.
A person knowing about Digital Logic Design is Eligible for this training on Programming Logic Controllers.
There will be an emphasis on the hardware and software interface of PLC.
This course aims to provide a strong foundation for students to excel in Control and Automation field.
Requirement
: Laptop/PC
Training Mode
: Online Lectures and Q&A sessions after every lecture.
Software
: GX Developer + Simulator (A download link will be provided)
