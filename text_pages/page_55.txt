Allen Bradley – RS Logix 5000 Training
It is a comprehensive course for learning the basic level programming of Allen Bradley PLC in RS Logix 5000 software. You will learn the logic designing of RS Logix 5000 from scratch in a short period. No hardware equipment will be required to follow the course. You will have to install RS Logix 5000 software on your PC and you are good to go. The fundamentals of RS Logix 5000 programming will be covered. It includes video lectures and assignments.
Buy Course
Course Introduction
This course is for Electrical, Electronics, Mechanical, or Mechatronics Engineers and Diploma holders.
A person knowing about Digital Logic Design is Eligible for this training on Programming Logic Controllers.
There will be an emphasis on the hardware and software interface of PLC.
This course aims to provide a strong foundation for students to excel in Control and Automation field.
Requirement
: Laptop/PC
Training
Mode: Online Lectures and Q/A sessions after every lecture.
Software
