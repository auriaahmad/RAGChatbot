Our Courses
We have created multiple online pieces of training for PLC programming. The courses include tasks, examples with solutions, and well-explained video lectures. People looking for a future in industrial automation and interested in learning  PLC and HMI programming can visit and enroll in the courses listed below to begin their careers in industrial automation.
Siemens - TIA Portal
It is a comprehensive course for learning the
basic level programming of Siemens S7 1200/1500 PLC in TIA Portal V15 software
. You will learn the logic designing of the TIA Portal from scratch in a short period. No hardware equipment is required to follow the course. You will have to install TIA Portal V15 software on the PC you are ready. The fundamentals of programming for the TIA Portal will be covered. Including the video lectures, assignments, and solved examples
