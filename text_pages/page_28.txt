Siemens – TIA Portal Training
It is a comprehensive course for learning the
basic level programming of Siemens S7 1200/1500 PLC in TIA Portal V15 software
. You will learn the logic designing of the TIA Portal from scratch in a short period. No hardware equipment is required to follow the course. You will have to install TIA Portal V15 software on the PC you are ready. The fundamentals of programming for the TIA Portal will be covered. Including the video lectures, assignments, and solved examples.
Course Introduction
Buy Course
This course is for Electrical, Electronics, Mechanical, or Mechatronics Engineers and Diploma holders.
A person knowing about Digital Logic Design is Eligible for this training on Programming Logic Controllers.
There will be an emphasis on the hardware and software interface of PLC.
This course aims to provide a strong foundation for students to excel in Control and Automation field.
Requirement
: Laptop/PC
Training Mode
: Online Lectures and Q&A sessions after every lecture.
Software: TIA Portal
+ Simulator (A download link will be provided)
Instructor:
Muhammad Raza Khalid
Graduate in Electronics Engineering.
Masters in Project Management.
5 years+ experience in the field of industrial automation.
